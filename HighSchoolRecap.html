New Hope Solebury High School

This is a high school located in New Hope, Pennsylvania. A beautiful little town just north of Philadelphia. There are about 500 student body, 40+ staff. The school representative color is blue and yellow, mascow is a lion. NHS a great academic standing and wonderful music program.
