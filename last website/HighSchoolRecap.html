New Hope Solebury High School

This is a high school located in New Hope, Pennsylvania. A beautiful little town just north of Philadelphia. There are about 500 student body, 40+ staff. The school representative color is blue and yellow, mascow is a lion. NHS a great academic standing and wonderful music program. A big shout out to Mr. Bateman who is retiring this year, he has brought lots of delightful music and joy in this little school. He changes the little heart who can go out and do great things. Another shout out to the cyber Web design class taught by Mr. McLuaghlin is definetly worth taking. Even one specific student is always slacking, but Mr. McLaughlin is still rocking out.

Mask & Zany

Mask & Zany is a theater club perform at Stephen J. Bucks theater in New Hope Solebury High School. Most years, Mask & Zany produce a Fall Play, Spring Musical, and a studio show in Late April. The heart of theater Melanie Pittner who also teaches AP Lang and Lit at the high school keep Mask & Zany running. She's a mom to all the kids in theater and really bring the show to life. Mask & Zany receive no funding from school, and it's self supporting from fund raising, donation, and voluntter work.
